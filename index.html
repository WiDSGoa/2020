About WiDS Goa

WiDS Goa is an independent event organized by Dikshita Dessai to coincide with the annual Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference held at Stanford University and an estimated 150+ locations worldwide. All genders are invited to attend WiDS regional events, which features outstanding women doing outstanding work.

WiDS Goa chapter is happy to have its first-ever conference on 28th February 2020 at CIBA Incubation Centre from 10:00AM to 1:00PM and at NIT Goa campus from 2:00PM to 05:00PM.
